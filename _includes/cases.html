Jack and the problems of counterfeiting

One day Jack decided to expand the product range and to sell branded bags, along with footwear. Without hesitation, he opened the b2b application to find suppliers and manufacturers working in the Elementh blockchain, and found bag manufacturer LOUIS VUITTON. He placed an order, transferring crypto currency to a smart contract. Having received information on Jack's order, the supplier formed a dispatch immediately and transferred data on serial numbers to the smart contract. Now that the transaction is underway, everyone will be able to verify that Jack has authentic LOUIS VUITTON bags in his store and not fake ones. This is because in the Elementh network each manufacturer can track the movement of goods and if several owners of the same serial number for one product appear, it means counterfeit, and its origin is very easy to track and take necessary measures to notify customers about its existence.
